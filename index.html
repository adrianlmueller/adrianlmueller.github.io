[About Me]

Hi, I’m Adrian! I’m a first-year ELLIS PhD student in Computer Science at ETH Zürich, fortunate to be advised by Niao He and co-advised by Gergely Neu. My research interests are the foundations of sequential decision-making (including reinforcement learning), online learning, and theoretical machine learning more broadly.

I obtained my MSc in data science and my BSc in mathematics, both from ETH. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I had been very fortunate to be supported by the German Academic Scholarship Foundation („Studienstiftung“). I had also been an active long-term member of the ETH Student Sustainability Committee.
